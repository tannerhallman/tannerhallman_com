Hello, I'm Tanner Hallman and I was born in Charlotte, North Carolina. I have always been deeply interested and invested in all the latest technology. From modifying and building computers, jailbraking phones (illegal?), and writing programs, my knowledge of computers has grown exponentially. It wasn't until after college, however, did I truely find my passion in the development world.

Android.

After graduating from UNCW with a degree in Business Administration: Marketing, I stumbled upon Android and I was immediately captivated. I had never before experienced a platform that provided the freedom and influence that Android cultivated. Over the past year, I have studied and practiced Android development through online courses, podcasts like Fragmented and Android Developers Backstage. I am incredible passionate about Android, and I can't wait to dive deeper into the world of mobile development.
